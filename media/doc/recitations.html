Recitation / Playing technique

These cards describe the way you should produce sound. The
normal letters describe the vocal production, the letters in italics
describe the instrumental production.

Voice:

SUNG means singing in stable pitches, as opposed to SPOKEN
which means unstable pitches./p>

VOWELS means singing or speaking only the vowels of the text/p>

CONSONANTS only the consonants (how to sing consonants ?
It will be a noisy sound with vocal chords, like a buzz or a
machine noise, depending on the consonants). Only if you see/p>

TEXT will you sing the poem as written.

VIBRATO 4 degrees: non vb, slow vib, vib and molto vib.

Instruments:

PITCH means relatively stable pitches from the 12-tone octave
whereas

OFFPITCH means hectically unstable and microtonal pitches.

CLEAN means harmonical sounds the instrument was initially
designed to make

NOISY means extended technique sounds that incorporate
non-harmonical elements

NOISE&CLEAN means alternating both modes of sound
production rapidly

Duration

These cards describe how long you should play before you look
at X and take away the next cards. The duration is either
measured in phrases or in notes. This, of course, implies no
absolute duration: you can leave a lot of silence between notes
or phrases.

Also, one special rule applies to repetitions: repeated phrases or
notes are counted only once. Thus you can, for example,
explore one 5 note phrase in may different rhythms, or create a
repetitive pattern, if needed. This rule could for example be
used if one of you needs to stop and create a new page: the
other could coast along on a few notes until the other is read to
play again.
